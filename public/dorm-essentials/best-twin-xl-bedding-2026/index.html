Buying Guide: What to Look For

Deep Pockets Matter

Dorm mattresses + toppers = 10-14" thick. Sheets need 16" pockets minimum.

Wash Before Use

Everything. Sheets, comforter, topper cover. Removes factory chemicals and pre-shrinks.

Corner Loops Save Hassle

Comforters with loops let you add a duvet cover later without the comforter bunching.
